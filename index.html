Our Story

StrikeEdge was born from a passion for cricket and a vision to empower every player with the perfect bat. Founded by Jinesh Patil, we noticed how many local cricketers struggled to find high-quality bats that matched their playing style.

We partnered with local wood crafters and former players to design bats that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern innovation. Each bat is carefully made, tested, and personalized, ensuring every player gets equipment that suits their technique and feel.

Our mission extends beyond just selling bats - we support young talent through affordable junior equipment and player sponsorships, helping build the future of cricket.
